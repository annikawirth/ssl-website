Let us organise your group trip to Lisbon!

A group, according to us, consists of four or more people. So if you are coming to Lisbon with friends, family, school, work or any other reason and you are 4 or more people, let us tailor your trip according to the size and style of your party.

Tours & Experiences

Choose from ready-made tours, like our street-art tour or hike to the christ or request a tailor-made tour, fit to your preferences.

Accommodation

We have trusted partnerships with hotels and hostels throughout Lisbon and will be more than happy to arrange your accommodation during your stay per request.

Food & Drinks

Lisbon’s is a gastronomical wonderland and we have created a food and wine tour to give you a perfect introduction: Lisbon food stories. We can also arrange meals in special places, wine tastings and food workshops.

Tours & Experiences

Bash Street Art

We’ll trace the course of the city’s street art from its origins in Bairro Alto through to its contemporary representation in LX Factory. We’ll even let you leave your own mark so you can be sure the city won’t forget you.

  • Spray Cans
  • Beer
  • All-knowing guide

 Price

Hike to the Christ

Adventurers unite! We’ll cross the river and roam through an abandoned fishing village before getting up close and personal with Jesus himself. This is a one-of-a-kind chance to experience a different side of Lisbon and take in the city’s majesty from a different point. Your trusty guide will lead you through this urban jungle with a backpack full of tasty snacks and a typical wine of the area and we’ll reward ourselves by wrapping up the day with a picnic on the water’s edge.

 Included

  • Transport
  • Picnic
  • Super-fit guide

 Duration

  • 3 Hours

 Price

  • On request according to group

Lisbon Food Stories

This gastronomical adventure combines some some truly mouth-watering dishes and delicious wines with impressive locations and mystical tales. We invite you to three of our favourite restaurants in an experience which allows Lisbon to sufficiently seduce you… Escape the tourist traps and enjoy a vast selection of traditional Portuguese food in our one-of-a-kind dining experience. Indulge all of your senses as you savour some perfectly salted bacalhau, watch the sun set over the water and get to know Lisbon’s gastronomical culture. The perfect opportunity to drink it all in… literally!

 Included

  • Food and Wine in 3 Restaurants
  • All-knowing guide

 Price

  • On request according to group
